CGCU Fresher’s Quest

Find Out More

So, you’ve made it to Imperial: Congratulations on this amazing achievement! If you study engineering at Imperial, you automatically become a member of the City and Guilds College Union.

As new Guildsmen and Guildswomen, it is important that you know where our office and home is. So we’ve put together this picture quest; all you need to do is find a sensible route to get from Imperial College’s main entrance to the CGCU Office.

Complete this quest and you can win points for your department in the CGCU Interdepartmental League.
