Welcome to the website of the City and Guilds College Union (CGCU) - the union for all Engineering students at Imperial College London!

If you are a Undergraduate Student at Imperial College London studying Aeronautics, Bio Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computing (including JMC), Design Engineering, Electrical and Electronic Engineering (including EIE), Mechanical Engineering, then you are both a member of Imperial College Union, and the CGCU.

We represent your Academic and Welfare needs to the College and Union, organise social events, work with CSP's including the 8 Dep Socs, and sell marvellous regalia.
