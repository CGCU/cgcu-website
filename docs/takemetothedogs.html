Where's the dogs?

The Dogs will be in the depths of the City and Guilds Building. But worry not, here is your handy guide in getting there!

Directions to CAGB 135a:

Start on Dalby Court. You're by the big blue building, looking towards main entrance.


Head down the stairs in front of you.


On your right, there is a door into a staircase. Go down to Level 1.


At Level 1, turn right through the grey doors.


Then, turn right again.


Head on down the corridor.


Go through these doors, on your left.


This what you will see. Turn left.


At the end of the mini-corridor, go through the double doors.


Congratulations, you've made it to CAGB 135a!
