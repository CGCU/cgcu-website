Congratulations on successfully completing the start of The Guildsheet BIG BRAIN TRAIL! You are about to embark on the quest of a lifetime, the pinnacle of your time at Imperial so far, for the title of the Biggest Brain on Campus (coz by default, engineers have a bigger brain than anyone else, ofc).

 Here are the basic competition details: 

This trail composes of various puzzles (surprise, surprise). You can work in groups of up to 3 people; alternatively, you can work alone. Solve the puzzles to compile the “Puzzle Code” as you go along the trail. 

 Before you start: SIGN UP using the Microsoft Form below. Once you have signed up, we will send you the first Puzzle Card, from there, make your way around campus solving the puzzles and compiling the puzzle code as you go. 

 Typically along with the puzzles, you’ll find the department and clue for the location of the next puzzle. At each location, you will find a step card, which will have a QR code which you can scan to get the next puzzle code. Alternatively, there is a link which you can plug into your browser and access the puzzle card there. 

Once you complete the code, send an email to us at guildsheet@ic.ac.uk with the subject as “BIG BRAIN TRAIL PUZZLE CODE”. Make sure your email has the following: 
• All team members’ names & short codes 
• Your Participation ID 
• The code (as a single string of CAPTIAL LETTERS [ABC] and NUMBERS [0-9])  
Once you send this through, we will email back one final task. 


Complete the final task first to win the prize of a £100 Amazon Gift Voucher! Simple.
 May the Biggest Brain Win !
