Events

We are planning some huge events this year. Check on our Facebook page for the latest info.

Last year, our Welcome Dinner was held at the grand Porchester Hall. The occasion saw more than 300 freshers wined and dined in style, as well as being entertained by our very own The Tectonics. If you don't know who they are, it can be said that they are the real-world Pitch Perfect, winning the a capella finals in New York in 2017.

We also hold a CGCU Festival every year to promote our values across the engineering departments at Imperial.
